Instructions and Helpful Tips for Filling Out the Criminal History and Background Check Form

Must be completed for any state lived since January 1st, 1988 or birth.

KidTraks will e-mail you for authorization after completion.

Should you have any questions, please contact HR at joinus@lastingchangeinc.org or your recruiter! We would be happy to assist you at any point in your hiring process.

HELPFUL HINTS: Common errors for criminal history form

  1. Your current address needs to include your street address – city, state, and zip code.
  2. “Date moved to this address” needs to have the month, day, and year.
  3. Section 2 (Please see example below): Residency needs city, state, and county (street address not required). This section must be done in chronological order. There may not be any gaps or overlap in history. You must go back to January 1, 1988 or birth date.

Example

(1) I was born 10/9/1990, I lived in my hometown Fort Wayne, IN until I went to college at (2) Indiana University! I always went (3) home for the summer. (4) In August 2009, I transferred home to finish my degree at IPFW and have lived in Fort Wayne since.
